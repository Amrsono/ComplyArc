ComplyArc is a state-of-the-art Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your

transforms complex compliance workflows into a streamlined, high-performance 
operational experience.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. CORE PLATFORM FUNCTIONALITIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[A] INTELLIGENT COMMAND CENTER (DASHBOARD)
The central nervous system of your compliance operations.
- Real-time KPI Tracking: Monitor Active Clients, Open Cases, and High-Risk alerts at a glance.
- Risk Distribution Heatmaps: Visualize portfolio risk by Country, Product Type, and Risk Factor.
- Interactive Analytics: Dynamic charts showing screening trends and case resolution velocity.
- Live Audit Feed: Secure, timestamped log of every interaction within the platform.

[B] HIGH-FIDELITY SCREENING ENGINE
Instant verification against global risk databases.
- Multi-Source Screening: Search PEP (Politically Exposed Persons), Sanctions, and Watchlists simultaneously.
- Fuzzy Matching Logic: Configurable sensitivity thresholds to reduce false positives while ensuring 100% coverage.
- Batch Processing: Upload and screen thousands of entities instantly via CSV/API.
- Direct-to-Case Workflow: Convert potential hits into active investigation cases with one click.

[C] CLIENT LIFECYCLE MANAGEMENT (CLM)
From onboarding to ongoing due diligence.
- Onboarding Wizard: Structured data collection for Individuals and Corporates.
- UBO Visualization: Interactive mapping of Ultimate Beneficial Ownership structures.
- Dynamic Risk Scoring: Automated risk calculation based on Country, Industry, and Interface factors.
- Profile Persistence: A "Golden Record" for every client with full document and note history.

[D] KANBAN CASE MANAGEMENT
Professional investigation environment for compliance teams.
- Visual Workflow: Transition cases through 'New', 'In-Progress', 'Review', and 'Resolved' statuses.
- Evidence & Notes: Secure internal communication thread for analysts to collaborate on investigations.
- Decision Audit Trail: Capture the 'Why' behind every case closure for regulatory review.

[E] CONTINUOUS MONITORING & ALERTS
Continuous vigilance that never sleeps.
- Real-time Watch: Automatically re-screens clients daily or upon external list updates.
- Smart Alerts: Intelligent prioritization of alerts by severity (High/Med/Low).
- Alert Resolution: Efficient queue management to clear harmless notifications quickly.

[F] AI-POWERED ADVERSE MEDIA
Reputational risk intelligence driven by Large Language Models.
- Global News Pulse: Scans thousands of global sources for negative mentions.
- Sentiment Classification: AI categorizes news into 'Financial Crime', 'Corruption', 'Legal Issues', etc.
- Smart Summaries: Instant AI-generated abstracts of complex news articles.

[G] COMPLIANCE REPORTING SUITE
Regulatory readiness in a single click.
- Template-Based Generation: Standard formats for SAR (Suspicious Activity Reports) and KYC audits.
- Export Capabilities: Professional PDF and Excel exports ready for submission to regulators.
