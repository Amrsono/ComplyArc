🟠 MODULE 3 — RISK ENGINE (CORE DIFFERENTIATOR)

You want a multi-factor risk model:

A. Client Risk (CRR)
High = 5
Medium = 3
Low = 1

Factors:

ownership complexity
UBO risk
sanctions exposure
PEP status
adverse media

B. Geography Risk (GRR)
country risk rating
FATF grey list / black list
corruption index
sanctions exposure
🧾 C. Product Risk (PRR)

Examples:

cash-intensive services → high
structuring companies → high
simple advisory → low
💻 D. Interface Risk (IRR)
direct client vs intermediary
remote onboarding
non-face-to-face onboarding

FINAL RISK SCORE FORMULA

You can define:

Total Risk Score =
(0.4 × Client Risk)
+ (0.2 × Geography Risk)
+ (0.2 × Product Risk)
+ (0.2 × Interface Risk)

Then normalize:

Score	Level
4.0 – 5.0	High
2.5 – 3.9	Medium
1.0 – 2.4	Low
